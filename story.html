"Witches are not born, they are created." This simple sentence is the answer to the question sorcerers have been trying to figure out for hundreds of years. Yet here it sits, taunting both them and me.

"First you must gather these ingredients and use them to make a paste." I felt awful for stealing this book from that strange old women, but after I started reading it I realized that if I knew that if I had known what was in it, I would have killed her to get this book.

"With that paste, you must draw this magic circle." This ritual could be a lie. This whole book could be a lie. Nothing could end up happening. Or doing this could kill me. I don't care. This is my last chance. If this doesn't work, I'm dead anyway. Nothing to lose and everything to gain.
